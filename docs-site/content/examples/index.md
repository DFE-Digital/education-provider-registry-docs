# Education Provider Registry Ontology — worked examples

Three examples showing how to represent different establishment types in the EPR ontology. Each draws on real GIAS data. All personal names are anonymised throughout.

| Example | Establishment or trust | Type |
|---|---|---|
| [Community school](./community-school/) | Argyle Primary School, URN 100008, Camden | LA-maintained, primary |
| [Academy](./academy/) | Abbey College, Ramsey, URN 137377, Cambridgeshire | Academy converter, secondary — establishment view |
| [Single-academy trust (SAT)](./sat/) | Abbey College, Ramsey (SAT) — Group ID TR00001, Companies House 07740516 | SAT trust — accountability and group membership |
| [Multi-academy trust (MAT)](./mat/) | Abbey Academies Trust (TR00261) with three Church of England primaries | MAT trust — trust identity and member academies |

Each example page includes a structural diagram, a namespace prefix block, and a series of focused Turtle snippets covering one aspect of the record at a time. The [community school example](./community-school/) also includes background on the ontology's design principles — OWL 2 punning, the use of blank nodes, and the absence of datatype properties.
